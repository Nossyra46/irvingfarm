Capucas, Honduras

Located in the dry south western hills of Honduras, Las Capucas is the model of a well run Cooperative. Their official name, Cooperativa Cafeteiera Capucas Limitada or COCAFCAL, is quite a mouthful, so we decided to pay homage to the whole community by calling this coffee simply "Capucas". The cooperative, run by Omar Rodriguez, utilizes modern washing and drying facilities to produce this fantastic coffee.
